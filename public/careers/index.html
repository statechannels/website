    ​About Mesh

    ConsenSys Mesh (a.k.a. MESH) is an accelerator, incubator, and investor of blockchain technology solutions. Our mission is to champion global adoption and awareness of groundbreaking technologies in Web 3.0.

    Founded in 2015 by Ethereum co-founder Joseph Lubin, MESH is a network of loosely coupled, tightly aligned teams, products, and investments that include:

    • A portfolio of 190 equity and token positions
    • Tachyon, our accelerator program for early stage Web 3.0 startups
    • Research & Development team making meaningful contributions to the Ethereum ecosystem
    • A suite of incubated companies such as ConsenSys Software, MetaMask., Gitcoin, Decrypt, Treum, and more

    We are a remote-friendly team with diverse backgrounds and a shared passion for advancing the arrival of Web 3.0. Given our rich history and founder focus we have built an unparalleled network and a rapidly growing organization. This is a remote role. To learn more visit https://mesh.xyz/ 

    About Magmo

    Magmo is a 4 person team focusing on Ethereum state channels research and implementation. Their main focus is the Nitro state channels  framework — a technique for executing transactions “off-chain”, reducing latency and cost.

    Over the past three years, the team has released a protocol specification, implemented clients in Typescript and Go, and built out proof-of-concept applications such as “web3Torrent”.  The next stage of the project is to deploy a network of clients in production — for example as a part of  the Filecoin Retrieval Market. To find out more, check out https://statechannels.org/.

    Magmo team members work remotely around the world but try to come together regularly at conferences and workshops.
